CitySpit

Functionality

Our app will provide brief real time information about the random cities. Information will include attractions to visit, news updates, and weather information about the cities. User will be able to save the cities based on interests. Later by accessing the liked list users will be ready to experience new adventures and have easy planning to favorite cities.

Purpose

Our app allows users to find new experiences randomly, which open doors to new adventures and new activities. Our goal is to deliver users informative platform to save time and plan their future travel easily.
